Beta Launch

Ungank is a collection of deep learning networks that accurately predict the positions of ALL players on a League of Legends map, including enemy players which would not normally be visible. These predictions are updated every second on a map displayed in a Firefox or Chrome web browser.

Ungank is free to use and runs from your web browser, with no plugins, logins, or software installation required. Screenshots of your game are collected using native browser methods.

Like any good copilot, Ungank is continuously learning from its experiences. The beta launch is gathering a huge amount of game data that will drastically improve game predictions over the next few months. Whether you're a casual player working on your map awareness or a veteran summoner looking for an edge, we hope to see you on the rift!
