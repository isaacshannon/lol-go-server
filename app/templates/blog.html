Man & Machine

Alpha Launch

Ungank is a collection of deep learning networks that accurately predict the positions of ALL players on a League of Legends map, including enemy players which would not normally be visible. Ungank runs in a mobile device's browser and uses the camera to grab screenshots of your computer's monitor. No installs, subscriptions or sign-ups are required.

Ungank is your League of Legends copilot, it's watching the map while you're concentrating on landing that skill shot. Like any good co-pilot Ungank is constantly learning and improving. The screenshots gathered during games are analyzed and the data added to the deep learning networks. Ungank users can expect a sharp increase in the accuracy of the predictions as more data are gathered.

Is Ungank cheating? We're not sure yet and we've asked Riot for guidance. Ungank is comparable to having a human coach sitting beside you and providing advice. Very few players can afford a coach, whereas Ungank is free for everyone and runs on any mobile device with access to the internet.
