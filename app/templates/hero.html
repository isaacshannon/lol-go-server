Tracking enemy players during a League of Legends match is challenging. Ungank uses a phone's camera to watch the map and predict enemy positions using deep learning networks.

Ungank is 100% free, runs from a phone's web browser, and requires no installation, sign-up or login.

Privacy

Ungank does NOT use cookies, or any other tracking mechanism.

The images of the in game map captured while using the app will be stored to train better predictive models. These images will never be sold or shared with other parties.

How Ungank Works

Ungank uses cutting edge deep learning techniques to crunch through hundreds of thousands of League of Legends maps and create a prediction model. The model can then predict likely enemy player locations, including those hidden by the fog of war.

The enemy positions are displayed as red grid squares on top of the current map. Grids which are very likely to have enemies are colored with a strong shade of red, while grids which are less likely to contain enemies are a pale red.
